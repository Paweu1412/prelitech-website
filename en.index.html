ABOUT US

Prelitech Spółka z o.o. was established as a result of structural changes in the business activities conducted since 1996, with the aim of strengthening the market position and further development. The direction of the Company's activities is assembly and electrical installation work in such industrial sectors as energy, oil and gas, metallurgy, chemistry and petrochemistry, environmental protection and other specialised industries. We carry out tasks where full professionalism is required in the execution and assembly of structures, technological installations, electrical installations, teletechnical infrastructure, automation, control and measurement.

Prelitech Spółka z o.o. carries out tasks both nationally and internationally. The company's potential is based on qualified engineering staff and an experienced team of employees. The way we are managed and the team of professionals who care about the quality of services provided are our calling card in business relations. Independent assembly brigades with professional tooling and workshop equipment and well-organised transport logistics make it possible to complete every task within the agreed deadline.
